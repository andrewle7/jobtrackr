Welcome to Job Trackr!

Job Trackr is a career service tool that supports users during their job search. Users can take a quiz that lets them know what entities they need on their resume, and they are provided with a score that shows what the what entities are missing from their resume. Users can also add jobs they have applied to, helping them keep track of applied jobs.
